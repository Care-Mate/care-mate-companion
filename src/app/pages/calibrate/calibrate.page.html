Pressure Pad Calibration

Place the sensor on an area indicated by the instruction manual. Pair the pulse oximetry monitor with the sensor. Place the strap across the patient’s lap and loosely attach it with the fasteners. Wait 1 minute for a reading. Watch the value for 10 seconds and if it hasn’t changed observe the value. If it’s at or below 92% press calibrate if not repeat tighten the strap to the next level and repeat the procedure. Repeat the process for all points indicated by the manual.
